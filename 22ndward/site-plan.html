Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a helpful and easier way to know the brothers and sisters of Riverton 22nd Ward, to know the leaders of each organization, have information about the next ward activities, and a remembrance of the past activities, also every month who is going to celebrate its birthday.

Audience

The audience is all the ward members, stakeholders, and people moving to the ward area. Also, it can contain information about sisters and elders serving a mission with messages that encourage us to serve, experiences, and testimonies of the ward members.

Content

Home page

Part of personal growth and progress in any sense includes knowing our roots, having our identity current is important, both as members of the church and in the same way the culture of the country from which each one comes. The culture and traditions of each country enrich the human being and allow him to have a broader perspective of himself, it helps to understand ourselves better and to develop a love like that of Jesus Christ. Being certain of our spiritual and earthly origin strengthens us and allows us to cultivate a stronger testimony, likewise as members of an LDS community and neighborhood it is relevant to develop a unity, a more personal contact, not only by attending Sunday services , but also to activities that help us to strengthen bonds of friendship. The testimonies, experiences, and messages of the leaders, together with the study of the Scriptures themselves, will help us cope with our daily personal challenges and collaborate with the members of the ward to be one in purpose and in faith. This will allow us to have a solid foundation to then effectively exercise our faith through concrete actions, both personally and as a family, and to work for those who have already left this world. Taking action is what will complement the foundation we forged. We hope that the material available on this website can contribute a little to its comprehensive development. We express our gratitude in advance for your collaboration to enrich this site and work together for our well-being.

Inspirational

Inspirational: People can visit this website without having an LDS account, if they are members of the church they can find some sections that may be of interest, and if they are not members of the church have an overview of the neighborhood, if they are new to the area or if you are choosing an area to move to and are interested in knowing the Latino ward that you could attend, it is a good tool to get an idea of ​​what this particular ward is like. It can serve to give the link as a reference to people who are not members of the church, so that they can learn about the church and even be encouraged to attend Sunday services or activities that are carried out. You will find different sections of inspiring messages and anecdotes from the members of the ward, quotes and messages from general leaders of the church. Ward history review: Location of the chapel and hours of services, also a brief history of the ward.

Taking action

Taking action: Ward activities.- They can know the activities have been carried out with a brief review and also know about upcoming events. They can know a description of the different organizations, how they are organized and their purpose, also know who the leaders of each one of them are. Study.- Information about the study classes of each month in the relief society and elders quorum. Genealogy.- To learn about genealogy, a section to explain what it is and why it is done. Include a last name genealogy section and a link to family search. Historical overview of the countries of the American continent. Birthday list.- You can find a monthly list of birthdays. It may be a good opportunity to start or resume ministry activities. Classifieds.- A section of classified ads in the neighborhood. Here you can post a general ad for neighborhood members that are personal. Contact information.- A contact section through a neighborhood email address or a telephone number to receive text messages, said emails or messages will be reviewed and channeled as appropriate.
